Visualizing the History of Pandemics

Details:

The visulization tells us about all the major pandemics that have occured throughout the human history and the total number of deaths caused by each pandemic.

I like this visualization because it is crisp and clear. The text is clearly visible and there is a clear change in the shape which tells us about the total number of deaths caused by the pandemic. Even though the color selection can be a minor downside of this visualization but the size of the shapes makes up for it.
